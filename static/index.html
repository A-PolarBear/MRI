Brain Tumor
MRI Segmentation

According to WHO, brain tumor is divided into non malignant and malignant brain tumor. Segmenting a image of brain is a critical step to make diagnosis and treatment plan. However, it's quite difficult and challenging to segment since the medical images are diffirent from the normal RGB images. Hence, we try to fully utilize the existed segmented brain tumor images and take the labled data in traget domain into accout to train our network, aiming at performing better than using unlabled target domain images singlely.

Our framework

Highlight💡

Utilize existed labeled data fully
Take the labeled images in target domain into account
Propose a novel framework on SSDA
Achieve a desirable outcome to help doctors make diagnosis
